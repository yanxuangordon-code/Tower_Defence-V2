TIPS & STRATEGIES

Frost + Tesla
Place Frost towers before Tesla. Slowed enemies take longer chains — massive damage boost.
🎯
Sniper Placement
Place Snipers on long straight sections of path. Their 7+ range covers more enemies per shot.
💰
Upgrade > Spam
A fully upgraded Cannon outperforms 3 basic ones. Upgrade towers already in good positions.
🔥
Inferno vs Tanks
Burn DoT ignores armor. Use Inferno against Tank and Armored enemies that resist other towers.
🚀
Missile Placement
Put Missiles at curves where enemies bunch up. Explosion radius hits multiple packed enemies.
💚
Kill Healers First
Medic enemies restore HP to nearby allies. Prioritize them or your DPS is wasted.
